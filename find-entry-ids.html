🔍 Form Entry ID Finder

Find and test your Google Form entry IDs

📋 Method 1: Using Browser Inspect (Recommended)

1 Open your Google Form in edit mode (add /edit to URL)

Example: https://forms.gle/9StjmPjZVzbD2sqJ7/edit

2 Right-click on the "Full Name" field → Click "Inspect" (or "Inspect Element")
3 Look for: name="entry.XXXXXXXXXX"

The numbers after "entry." are your entry ID. Write it down!

4 Repeat for all fields:
Full Name → entry.???? Email → entry.???? Phone → entry.???? Your Age → entry.???? Guardian/Parent Name → entry.???? Preferred Batch → entry.???? School Name → entry.????

🔗 Method 2: Using Page Source

1 Open your Google Form: https://forms.gle/9StjmPjZVzbD2sqJ7

(NOT the /edit link, just the regular form)

2 Press Ctrl+U (or Cmd+U on Mac) to view page source
3 Press Ctrl+F and search for: entry.
4 You'll see all entry IDs. Match them to your fields by looking at nearby text
