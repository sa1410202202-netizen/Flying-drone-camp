📚 Curriculum Highlights

Day 1: Introduction to Drones

Learn drone components, how they work, and basic physics of flight. Safety briefing and equipment familiarization.

Day 2: Basic Flight Controls

Master throttle, pitch, roll, and yaw controls. Get comfortable with flight simulation before hands-on flying.

Day 3: Advanced Techniques

Learn acrobatic maneuvers, precision flying, and obstacle navigation. Fly through challenging courses.

Day 4: Racing & Competitions

Participate in speed races, target hits, and creative challenges. Compete with fellow pilots.

Day 5: Final Project & Showcase

Complete final challenges, receive certificates, and showcase your skills in the closing ceremony.
