✈️ Registration Verification System

📝 Step 1: Fill and Submit the Form

1 Go to Registration Page: registration.html
2 Fill all fields:
  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Age
  • Guardian/Parent Name
  • Preferred Batch
  • School Name
3 Click "Submit Registration" button
4 You should see: "✅ Registration submitted successfully!"

💾 Step 2: Check Submission was Received (Local)

First, let's verify your browser received the data:

🖥️ Step 3: Check Browser Console for Details

Open Browser Developer Tools:
Press F12 on your keyboard
Go to "Console" tab

You should see messages like:
📤 Submitting registration: {...}
💾 Saved to local storage
✅ Successfully sent to Google Form

📊 Step 4: Check Your Google Sheet

1 Open your Google Sheet:
📊 Click Here to Open Google Sheet
2 Look for "Form Responses 1" sheet tab at the bottom
3 Check if new rows appear with your registration data
4 Wait 30-60 seconds if data doesn't appear immediately

📋 Step 5: Check Google Form Responses

1 Open your Google Form:
📋 Click Here to Open Google Form
2 Click "Responses" tab at the top
3 You should see your submission listed
4 Click green Sheet icon to see all data in table format

📥 Step 6: View All Submissions & Download

✅ Complete Checklist

  • Form fills without errors
  • Submit button shows "Registration submitted successfully"
  • Browser console shows "📤 Submitting registration"
  • Local storage shows your submission (use "Check Local Submissions")
  • Google Sheet shows new row after 30-60 seconds
  • Google Form responses tab shows your data
  • Dashboard displays your submission

❓ Troubleshooting

Problem: Data not appearing in Google Sheet?

  • Wait 60 seconds - Google Forms takes time to sync
  • Refresh your Google Sheet (F5)
  • Check other sheet tabs - might be on different tab
  • Check "Form Responses 1" tab specifically
  • Verify form was actually connected to this sheet

Problem: Browser console shows no messages?

  • Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  • Click "Console" tab
  • Fill the form again and submit
  • Look for our logged messages
